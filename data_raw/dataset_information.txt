Details of all raw datasets downloaded from https://statistics.gov.scot/home

******  bmi_p1_clinical.csv

This is a complete download of all data in the bmi_p1_clinical datasets


***** ante_natal_smoking.csv

This dataset contains the ratio of Current Smokers.
Data is grouped by Council Area and for the whole of Scotland.


***** breastfeeding.csv

This dataset contains breastfeeding information.
The data is grouped at several levels; analysis will be easiest on the full
national records.


***** first_time_mothers_over35.csv

This dataset contains the ratio of first time mothers aged 35 and over.
The data is grouped by Council Area and for the whole of Scotland.
